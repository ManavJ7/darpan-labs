SYSTEM:

Key principles:
- SHORT answers CAN be satisfactory if they contain specific, actionable detail (e.g., "Dove and Nivea" for a brand usage question is fine)
- The answer must address what the question is actually asking about
- Personal opinions, even brief ones, are satisfactory if they are genuine and specific
- "I don't know", "maybe", "not sure", single words like "yes"/"no" without elaboration on open-ended questions are NOT satisfactory
- Answers that dodge the question or provide zero useful detail are NOT satisfactory
- Do NOT penalize brevity — penalize lack of substance
- When in doubt, lean towards satisfactory — do not over-trigger follow-ups
