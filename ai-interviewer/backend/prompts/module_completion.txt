determine if the module's coverage and confidence thresholds are met.

DEVELOPER:
Module: {module_id} - {module_name}
Required signals: {signal_targets}
Completion criteria: coverage >= {coverage_threshold}, confidence >= {confidence_threshold}

Questions and answers so far:
{module_turns}

=== EVALUATION CRITERIA ===

Signal counting rules:
- A signal counts as "captured" if its confidence >= 0.4
- A signal mentioned multiple times across answers should have HIGHER confidence (evidence accumulates)
- Weighted scoring: not all signals are equal. Core signals for the module matter more than edge signals.

Coverage calculation:
- coverage_score = (number of captured signals) / (total required signals)
- Only count signals with confidence >= 0.4 as captured

Confidence calculation:
- confidence_score = average confidence across ALL required signals (including missing ones as 0.0)
- This means missing signals actively drag down the confidence score

=== MULTI-FACTOR EVALUATION ===

In addition to signal coverage and confidence, evaluate twin readiness across these dimensions:

1. **narrative_depth_score** (0-1): How many concrete episodes and anecdotes were captured?
   - 0.0-0.3: Mostly abstract/generic answers, few specific stories
   - 0.4-0.6: Some concrete examples but missing depth
   - 0.7-1.0: Rich with specific anecdotes, episodes, and emotional moments

2. **style_coverage_score** (0-1): Can we replicate their communication voice?
   - 0.0-0.3: Not enough data to characterize their style
   - 0.4-0.6: Some style markers observed but not consistent
   - 0.7-1.0: Clear communication patterns, vocabulary tendencies, and hedging/directness style

3. **contradiction_count**: Number of discovered tensions between answers. This is POSITIVE — contradictions make twins more realistic and human.

4. **twin_readiness_score**: Composite score calculated as:
   `0.4 * coverage_score + 0.25 * confidence_score + 0.2 * narrative_depth_score + 0.15 * style_coverage_score`

=== RECOMMENDATION LOGIC ===

COMPLETE — recommend when:
- twin_readiness_score >= 0.65
- OR coverage >= threshold AND confidence >= threshold (legacy path)
- OR coverage >= threshold with only 1-2 low-priority signals missing and confidence is close to threshold (within 0.05)
- The user has provided enough information to build a meaningful profile for this module

ASK_MORE — recommend when:
- twin_readiness_score < 0.65 AND important signals are still missing
- narrative_depth_score < 0.4 (need more concrete episodes)
- The user seems engaged and willing to continue
- Fewer than 8 questions have been asked
- Include 2-3 specific suggested_next_questions targeting the most important missing signals

SKIP_OPTIONAL — recommend when:
- More than 12 questions have been asked and coverage gains are diminishing
- Only low-priority signals remain and the user seems fatigued
- The cost of additional questions outweighs the marginal signal value
