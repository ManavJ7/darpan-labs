## Two-Pass Review

### Pass 1: Structural Validation
Check each field against these rules:
1. Core Insight must be 25-150 words, written in consumer voice (not brand-speak), and express a genuine human truth.
2. Big Idea must be 25-150 words, distinct from the insight, and describe the creative concept in plain language.
3. Key Message must be a single line that a consumer would remember.
4. Execution Sketch must be 3-5 sentences describing what a finished ad might look like.
5. Flag brand-speak ("disruptive", "best-in-class", "world-class", "revolutionary", "game-changing") and suggest consumer-language rewrites.
6. Flag if the Big Idea is too abstract to visualize and suggest concretization.
7. Check distinctiveness: flag if this territory is substantially similar to another territory in the study.
8. **Product fit check**: flag if the territory doesn't fit the product in the Product Brief (e.g., a "luxury indulgence" territory for a budget product). Use flag name "product_mismatch".
9. **Must-communicate check**: flag if the territory fails to communicate the Product Brief's `must_communicate` message. Use flag name "misses_must_communicate".
10. **Brand personality check**: flag if the territory's tone/execution contradicts the brand personality defined in the Study Brief (`brand_personality_traits` in the study brief). Use flag name "personality_conflict".

### Pass 2: Polish
For each field, suggest tighter wording that:
- Sharpens the human truth in Core Insight
- Removes brand-speak
- Makes the Big Idea more concrete and visual
- Ensures the Key Message has stopping power
